Our Story

Adarsha Hindu Hotel is inspired by the journey and values of Hajari Thakur,the protagonist of author Bibhutibhushan Bandopadhyay's novel "Adarsha Hindu Hotel" whose dedication to honest cooking and discipline made his humble establishment a symbol of trust.

We bring that same spirit to our kitchen, serving authentic Bengali meals prepared with care, simplicity, and tradition.

Our aim is to offer a warm, homely dining experience where every guest feels welcomed and satisfied.
